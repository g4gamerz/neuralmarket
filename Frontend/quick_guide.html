This guide will help you get started with analyzing stock sentiment using our platform. NeuralMarket leverages AI to provide insights into news surrounding Pakistani stocks.

Getting Started

Using NeuralMarket is straightforward. Hereâ€™s a step-by-step guide:

  • Navigate to the Analysis Tool: From your dashboard, click on "Analysis Tool" in the navigation bar or the prominent "Analyze Stock Sentiment" card.
  • Enter a Stock Symbol: In the input field, type the PSX stock symbol you're interested in (e.g., LUCK, HBL, OGDC). Our system works best with official symbols.
  • Click "Analyze": Once you've entered the symbol, click the "Analyze" button.

Understanding the Results

After a brief processing time, you'll see the analysis results, which include:

  • Stock Details: The company name and the symbol you queried.
  • Current Price: The latest available price for the stock (data provided by EODHD).
  • Overall News Sentiment: An aggregated sentiment (Positive, Negative, or Neutral) based on recent news, along with a visual sentiment bar.
  • Recent News Headlines: A list of recent news articles related to the stock. Each headline will show:
    • Its individual sentiment classification (Positive, Negative, Neutral, or N/A if undetermined).
    • The sentiment score (typically ranging from -1 for very negative to +1 for very positive).
    • The publisher and publication date.
    • A link to read the full article.
  • News Sentiment Distribution: A bar chart showing the number of recent articles classified as Positive, Neutral, and Negative.

Interpreting Sentiment Scores

The sentiment score provides a numerical representation of the sentiment:

  • Positive Scores (e.g., > 0.15): Indicate generally positive news sentiment.
  • Negative Scores (e.g., < -0.15): Indicate generally negative news sentiment.
  • Neutral Scores (around 0): Indicate neutral or mixed sentiment, or that the model couldn't determine a strong sentiment from the text.

The overall sentiment label is determined by looking at the majority of article sentiments and the average score. Remember, this is an AI-driven analysis and should be used as one of many tools for your investment research, not as sole financial advice.

Exporting Reports

You can export the current analysis view as a PDF document for offline reference:

  • After an analysis is displayed, click the "Export Report (PDF)" button located in the "Stock Details" card.
  • Your browser will then download the generated PDF file.

Tips for Best Results

  • Use Official Stock Symbols: For the most accurate price data and targeted news, use the official PSX ticker symbols.
  • Consider Multiple Factors: Sentiment analysis is a powerful tool, but always consider it alongside other fundamental and technical analysis before making investment decisions.
  • Check Publication Dates: Pay attention to the dates of the news articles to understand the timeliness of the sentiment.
